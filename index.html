Bringing hope and wholeness to Kitsap County and beyond!

We believe in the life-saving love of Jesus & desire to share it with YOU!

Please join us on-line or in person where we discuss biblical topics together to better understand the great love God has for us.

  • 10:00AM We offer 3 different small-group bible studies geared for adults that follow an Adventist bible study guided known as a Quarterly. There are also weekly classes available for children.

  • 11:00AM Congregrational worship with prayers and praise music followed by a sermon given by Pastor Baker or other guest speakers.
