Groups & Seminars

Jesus can change your life! Jesus brings healing from pain, peace from stress, joy from depression, hope from `fear, freedom from bad habits, forgiveness from failure, and love from anger. All of these come as we grow closer Jesus. We regularly offer groups and seminars to help you (and everyone in our community) experience Jesus’ hope and wholeness.
